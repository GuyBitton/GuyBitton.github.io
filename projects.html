A Low-Cost Miniature 2-DoF Linear Actuator for Haptic Interfaces

  • Rapid Prototyping
  • Solidworks
  • Electronics
  • Mechanical Analysis
  • Haptics

Designed and built a 2-DoF linear actuator for tactile haptic interfaces. This actuator is as small as 60x60x35 mm but can produce a thrust force of up to 180 N (~18 Kgf or 40 lbf) with a resolution of 0.0136 mm per motor revolution. Its affordable and effective design (3D printed parts and low-cost DC motors) together with its high translational accuracy make it ideal for low-budget research applications such as haptic devices. This design was published in the IEEE journal Transactions on Haptics.
